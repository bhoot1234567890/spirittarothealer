FAQs

Common Questions

help
What is a tarot reading? expand_more

A tarot reading is a spiritual consultation where cards are drawn to gain insights into your life's questions. The cards reveal patterns, influences, and potential outcomes based on ancient wisdom and intuitive interpretation.

How accurate are tarot readings? expand_more

Tarot readings provide guidance and insights based on your current energy path. The accuracy depends on how open you are to receiving the message. Think of it as a mirror reflecting your subconscious thoughts and feelings.

What should I ask during a reading? expand_more

Come with specific questions about areas of your life where you seek clarity - relationships, career, health, or spiritual growth. The more focused your questions, the more precise the guidance will be.

How does the payment work? expand_more

Payment is done after the session, based on trust and satisfaction. This ensures you can fully receive the reading without worry. Payment details are shared via UPI or bank transfer after your session completes.

Is everything confidential? expand_more

Absolutely. Your reading and personal information are completely confidential. What is shared during the session stays between us, honoring the sacred trust between seeker and reader.

Can I record my reading? expand_more

Yes! You're welcome to take notes or record the session (with permission) so you can revisit the insights later. Many clients find it helpful to reflect on the guidance in the days following their reading.
